About Me

Hi, my name is Alexandra and I’m bringing a new perspective to the glossy, fine-tuned aesthetics of food photography.

I like to combine my creativity and talents in art vision to every aspect of photography, styling and art direction. I love to work with big and small clients, as long as there are new challenges and ideas – I am always into curious, shiny people and creative projects.
